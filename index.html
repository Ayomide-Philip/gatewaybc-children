Meet Our Children’s Ministry Team

Our Leaders

At Gateway Baptist Church, our Children’s Ministry is blessed with a dedicated team of leaders who are passionate about nurturing the spiritual growth of our children. Meet the wonderful people who guide and inspire our kids each week:

Rved Miss Florence Oluwakemi Adedeji - Children’s Ministry Director

Rved Miss Florence Oluwakemi Adedeji has been leading our Children’s Ministry for quite long number years. She Worked at ebenezer baptist high school, Ibadan, Studied at The Nigerian Baptist Theology Seminary Ogb, Went to polytechnic high school ibadan. She is committed to creating a loving and engaging environment for all children.

Sunday School Teachers

Our Sunday School teachers bring a wealth of experience and enthusiasm to their roles, creating a dynamic and engaging learning environment for our children.

  • Preschool: Our preschool teachers have a deep understanding of early childhood education. Their creativity and enthusiasm make learning about Jesus fun for our youngest members. They enjoy and often incorporate their special talents into their lessons to make them more engaging.

  • Elementary: Our elementary teachers have a heart for teaching and a love for God’s Word. They help our elementary students understand and apply biblical truths. Known for their patience and kindness, they are favorites among the kids and are dedicated to fostering a sense of community within the classroom.

Volunteer Coordinators

Our volunteer coordinators play a crucial role in ensuring our ministry runs smoothly. They organize and support our wonderful volunteers, connecting with both parents and volunteers alike. With their skills in event planning, they ensure everyone feels welcomed and valued. Outside of church, they enjoy gardening and are passionate about community service initiatives.

The History of the Children Ministry

In the Scripture, the Assembly of Gods people compromised of adults(Men & Women), Youths and Children. Let take a look at the history of the children ministry.As it was recorded in Deuteronomy 31:11-12.

The Church started in 1960 with the combination of adults and children worshipping together. This continued till 1966 when Ojesinas and others joined the church. Though there were some disadvantage in bringing adults and children together under the same roof for worship, learning the samethings, and even listing to the same lecture and Sermons; nevertheless, the disadvantages are more than the advantages.

Early in the 1973, a building was erected for the children, and other workers joined them. The first building was erected on the Nigerian Railways land. In May, a final court verdict to demolish the erected building on the land was given. On 1st August 2009, the building was demolished and a new one was proposed to be built on the piece of land that the church acquired very near the main church auditorium.

On 3rd August 2009, the new Children Church Foundation was laid. The following people took part in the Foundation laying service: Revd Dr. A.A. Alade, Revd E. Ade Adedeji,and Pastor (Mrs.) F.O. Adedeji, Pastor Ranti Amoo, Pastor J.A Adesope, Revd Dr. Olaniyan (The Baptist Conference Secretary of Oyo West), Mrs. Alao, Mr. Ogundeji, Juwon Adedeji, Precious Ayankeye and others. The building was completed to a stage and was dedicated temporarily for use October 26th by some members led by Revd Dr. A.A Alade before the celebration of the Goodness of God that took place that sunday.
